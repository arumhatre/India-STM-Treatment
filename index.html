FAQs

What is STM hair therapy, and how does it work?

STM (Stem Cell Transdermal Method) Hair Therapy is a safe and non-invasive treatment that uses plant-based growth factors and multivitamins to rejuvenate your scalp and promote healthy hair growth. It nourishes hair follicles from within, enhancing hair thickness and volume.

Is STM hair therapy painful?

Not at all. STM therapy is designed to be completely comfortable. The procedures, like laser stimulation and serum application, are painless and relaxing—many patients describe it as a soothing experience.

How soon will I see results?

Most patients notice visible improvement in hair density and quality within 10 weeks. Consistency and regular sessions are key to achieving the best outcomes.

Is STM therapy suitable for everyone?

STM is ideal for individuals experiencing moderate to severe hair loss due to genetics, stress, hormonal imbalances, or environmental factors. During your consultation, our experts will assess your condition and determine if this treatment is suitable for you.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your hair loss severity and goals. Typically, a treatment plan consists of 10–12 sessions spaced over several weeks for optimal results.

Are there any side effects?

STM Hair Therapy uses plant-based ingredients and advanced technology, ensuring it is safe and free from side effects. It’s a gentle, non-invasive method suitable for sensitive scalps.

Can STM therapy work for genetic hair loss?

Yes, STM is particularly effective for managing genetic hair loss. It stimulates dormant follicles, strengthens existing hair, and improves overall scalp health.

Can I combine STM therapy with other hair loss treatments?

Yes, STM can be combined with other treatments, such as homeopathic remedies or topical applications, to enhance results. Your doctor will guide you based on your specific needs.

How long does each session take?

A typical STM session lasts about 45–60 minutes, making it convenient even for those with busy schedules.

Do I need to make lifestyle changes during the treatment?

While STM therapy addresses hair loss directly, maintaining a healthy lifestyle—balanced diet, stress management, and proper scalp care—can further enhance results.

Is STM therapy a permanent solution?

STM therapy stimulates long-lasting hair growth, but maintaining results may require periodic sessions, depending on the underlying cause of your hair loss. Regular follow-ups can help sustain progress.

How much does STM Hair Therapy cost?

The cost varies based on the number of sessions and the treatment plan recommended for you. A detailed breakdown will be provided during your consultation.

What makes STM therapy different from other hair loss treatments?

STM combines advanced French technology with plant-based ingredients, offering a safe, non-invasive approach to hair restoration without the risks of surgical procedures.

Can women undergo STM therapy?

Absolutely! STM therapy is effective for both men and women experiencing hair thinning or hair loss.

How do I get started with STM Hair Therapy?

Book a consultation with us to evaluate your hair and scalp condition. Our experts will design a personalised treatment plan for your needs.
